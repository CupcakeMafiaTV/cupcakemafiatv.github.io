Hello! I'm Cody, a variety live streamer from Canada and the self proclaimed First Try King. In between countlessly quoting The Simpsons and my other favorite TV sitcoms, I'm typically found bouncing between playing several different competitive style games such as League of Legends or Fortnite while continuing to try and obtain 100% completion and achievements within my endlessly large Steam library. By day I'm just your average computer technician but by night I'm your much inferior trashcan teammate that always seems to let you down within competitive games. As someone who enjoys tinkering with technology, combining that skillset with my overall love of gaming has made streaming a perfect match.

Beyond streaming, I’m a massive collector of nerdy memorabilia, Funko POPs and trading cards and spend a lot of my free time looking for my next sought-after treasure to add to my collection. I’m a big fan of DC Comics and the Batman Universe especially Punchline & Harley Quin and is most likely the only person that actually enjoyed the game Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League. Some of my other all time favorite fandoms include Scott Pilgrim vs. The World, WWE/Wrestling, Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh & Digimon.

FAQ:

How long have you been streaming?
My first ever live stream occured on Dec 10th, 2017 and I have been streaming on and off ever since. Since May 2024, I have been taking streaming and content creation a lot more seriously and look forward to seeing just how far we can grow as a community.
What type of things to do you collect?
I have always been a collector of various trinkets and gadgets. At the moment I mostly enjoy collecting Funko POPs, wrestling action figures, Pokémon cards, CGC graded comics, retro video games and much more! To see the full extent of my collection, make sure to check out my Instagram.
Who does all of the art for your channel?
All of my channel art is done by Sketch Farm. She does absolutely phenomenal work and you should totally consider reaching out if you're in the need of any custom graphics.
Pineapple on pizza or nah?
Any form of pineapple on pizza such as Hawaiian or any other variation is tolerable but is not going too top my all time great pizza combination list.
Do you watch anime?
As of late, I started getting a lot more into anime! As a child, I was addicted to watching Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon & Sailor Moon but it eventually fell out of favor. Recently I watched Spy x Family with a friend and absolutely loved it which really reignited my desire to watch more. The next big anime that I have on my watch list is Demon Slayer but I'm always open to new suggestions for what I should watch next!
Do you have any favorite games?
Yes! My favorite game of all time is Scott Pilgrim vs. the World: The Game. Although some additional favorite games franchises are Life Is Strange, The Shantae Series, Mafia, Halo, League of Legends, World of Warcraft, Grounded & Genshin Impact.
