About Me

I'm the self proclaimed First Try King, achievement hunting enthusiest, The Simpsons historian, the one guy that watches anime in dubbed and most likely the only person on the planet that actually really enjoyed Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League. By day I'm just an average computer technican, while by night I'm your much infieriour teamate that always seems to let you down within competitive games. As someone who enjoys tinkering with technolgy, combining that skillset with my overall love of gaming has made content creation/streaming a match made in heaven.


While I enjoy and play all different types of games, I typically tend to stick to mostly playing MMORPGs, MOBAs, FPS, Monster Catching, Sports and Adventure games. I'm also a massive geek surrounding the fandoms of Scott Pilgrim vs. The World, The Shantae video games series, DC Comics (especially The Joker, Harley Quinn, & Punchline), WWE/Wrestling, Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh & Digimon
